Buy:-

As one of India’s premium auction houses, we offer collectors an opportunity to bid on a range of categories including Modern and Contemporary Indian Art, European Classics, Medieval Masterpieces, Renaissance Revival, Neoclassical Creations, Romanticism in Art,Realism and Naturalism, Post-Impressionist Wonders, Art Deco Marvels, Mid-Century Modern, Contemporary European Art.we aim to provide every client with the best buying experience possible.

Why Vadickala?
  • Year-Round Opportunities: Vadickala hosts round-the-year auctions spanning a diverse range of categories, ensuring you have multiple chances to discover and acquire unique pieces.
  • Specialized Teams: Each category is curated by specialists with extensive market insights and years of experience, offering you a nuanced understanding of the items featured in our auctions.
  • Research-Driven Confidence: At Vadickala, our commitment to a research-based approach and a team of experts ensures that you can bid with confidence, knowing that each item's authenticity and value are thoroughly vetted.
  • Effortless Bidding Experience: Enjoy a quick and convenient bidding process through our user-friendly website. Vadickala is dedicated to providing a seamless and hassle-free platform for your bidding journey.
  • Secure Transactions: Your security is our priority. Benefit from secure payment gateways and reliable shipping services, ensuring that your acquisitions reach you in pristine condition.

Our Process:
  • Thorough Examination: Our specialists rigorously examine each item to guarantee authenticity and quality before making them available for auction.
  • Ownership Verification: We conduct a meticulous review of ownership history, supporting documentation, and relevant databases, ensuring a smooth and secure transfer of ownership.
  • Market Valuation: Vadickala determines the market worth of each item, establishing an estimate range based on our expertise. This transparent valuation process provides you with valuable insights during live bidding.
  • Exclusive Access: Gain entry to a world of exclusive paintings, including works from emerging artists not available anywhere else.
  • Virtual Art Exhibitions: Immerse yourself in the art world through Vadickala's virtual exhibitions, exploring collections and attending artist interviews.
